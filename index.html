Welcome to LAU's Innovation center

A place where ideas are born

Our purpose

Our purpose is promoting a culture of innovation, supporting a major change in the curriculum, and revolutionizing the mode of education delivery in addition, this center is built to to equip our students with the necessary tools to succeed in today’s increasingly competitive market.

Our plan

The center will focus on initiating training programs, competitions, ideation sessions, and hackathons, among others, that will target LAU students, staff, and alumni, and the community at large. Our center will also serve as an incubator that seeds innovative ideas and prepares them for acceleration and start-ups.

Our vision

“Innovation is an essential 21st century skill needed for success at work in a global economy impacted by the fourth industrial revolution. Accordingly, students need to develop an innovative mindset that includes creativity, critical thinking, flexibility, and complex problem solving.”

Provost George Nasr
